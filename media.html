Corrected Record Summary

The 2005 antique-money case is often reduced to early arrest coverage. Later public coverage reported that larceny charges were dropped and that prosecutors would no longer pursue the case. This site links to both the original reporting and the later coverage so readers can see the full timeline.

The OSHA-related articles are also incomplete without the later First Circuit update. In Barry Billcliff v. Lori Chavez-DeRemer, Secretary of Labor, No. 23-2082, the First Circuit addressed the petition-for-review issue under 29 U.S.C. Sec. 660(a) and stated that "The petition is ALLOWED."
